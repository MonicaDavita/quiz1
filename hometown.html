SURABAYA

ꦱꦸꦫꦧꦪ

Founded over 900 years ago on the island of Java, it was one of the first port cities of Southeast Asia. Today it is the second-largest city in Indonesia, second only to the capital, Jakarta.

A vibrant, sprawling metropolis, it mixes modern skyscrapers with canals and buildings from its Dutch colonial past.

Surabaya is known as Kota Pahlawan (the city of heroes) due to the importance of the Battle of Surabaya during the Indonesian National Revolution.
